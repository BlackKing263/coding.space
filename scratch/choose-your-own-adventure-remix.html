Choose Your Own Adventure

In this project, we're going to build a app where we talk to a character of your creation and go on an adventure with them. You should make your character and story original and your own. Feel free to embellish on the storyline as you see fit.

1) Create a new project with an interesting background an character.

2) On flag click, have your character say "Click me to say hi"

3) On click, make your character say hi, ask for the user's name, and store it in a variable called "name".

4) Say hi to the user by name.

5) Ask the user how they're doing and respond based on their answer.

6) Ask the user if they're ready for an adventure, and give them two options of different places to go. Make different scenarios for both of the options. You could go to the moon, to help Harry Potter defeat Voldemort, to steal candy from Candy Land, to battle a Sith Lord. It's up to you!

7) Once you are happy with your game, share it publicly by clicking the share button in the top-right corner and submit it below.
