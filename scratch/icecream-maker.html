Ice Cream Maker

In this project, we're going to build a game where you need to make the ice cream orders of picky customers!

1) Create an animation letting the player know what they are going to do in the game.

2) Have your backdrop switch to a station for ice cream making.

3) Create ice cream and at least three toppings and make them start at the station when the flag is clicked. Bonus: Create two flavors of ice cream.

4) Make a bowl to catch your toppings. Have it begin at the front of the station and slowly move across the station. When it reaches the end of the station have it go back to the beginning and start again.

5) Have your ice cream and toppings fall when clicked. If they land in your bowl, have them follow the bowl but return to the starting position if they miss the bowl. Bonus: Create an animation for falling icecream and toppings.

6) Now we need to make the orders. Create a list that randomly chooses icecream and if people want toppings, make sure an ice cream is always picked. When the bowl has reached the end of the station have the items go back to their starting points, and a new order created.

7) Create a new list that keeps track of what you have in your bowl. Create a score variable that keeps track of the correct orders.

8) Create a variable that keeps track of incorrect orders. Have the game end if there are too many incorrect orders.

Bonus: Make customers
