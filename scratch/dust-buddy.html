Dust Buddy

The Coding Space Project

How long can you keep your Dust Buddy safe from the impending doom of the Vacuum?

1) First, pick, or draw, your character that is going to be chased, and pick a backdrop.

2) Next, get this character to follow your mouse around.

3) Time to add another character and make it chase your original character.

4)When the character is caught by the chaser, indicate that the game is over with an animation.

5)Add a variable (in Data) that shows how long the user lasted before the sprites touched.

6) Challenge step: Can you make your game keep track of the user that has the high score? (Hint: you’ll need a cloud variable. Ask your instructor to help you become a Scratcher.)

7) Share your game publicly by clicking the share button in the top-right corner and submit it below.
