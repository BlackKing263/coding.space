Calculator

In this project, we'll make a simple old-fashioned calculator that can do addition, subtraction, multiplication, devision, and more.

For an example, click here
  1. Add a title to your page.
  2. Create a series of buttons for the number pad.
  3. Create a space for the screen.
  4. Whenever a number or operation is clicked, add that to the screen.
  5. Create a delete button and have it delete one character at a time from the screen when clicked.
  6. Make the equals button work. Hint: check out eval()
  7. Feel free to customize your calculator. Make it a tip calculator. Or make it do math incorrectly. Continue working until you are happy with your project.
  8. Publish your website and submit the link below.
